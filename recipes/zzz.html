Zzz Tea

Ingredients

  • Boiling Water
  • Chamomile Leaves
  • Lavender Buds
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Place Chamomile Leaves and Lavender Buds in Tea Bag.
  2. Pour Boiling Water over Tea Bag steap for 4 minutes.
  3. Remove Tea Bag and mix in Honey with a spoon.
  4. Enjoy with friends if possible.
