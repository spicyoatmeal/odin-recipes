Revival Tea

Ingredients

  • Boiling Water
  • Thin Lemon Slices
  • Honey
  • Mint Leaves

Instructions

  1. Drizzle Honey on the bottom of tea pot.
  2. Place Lemon Slices & Mint Leaves on top of honey.
  3. Gently press down on Lemon SLices & Mint with spoon to release juice/ aroma.
  4. Pour Boiling Water into Tea Pot
  5. After 4 minutes pour into tea cups with strainer and enjoy!
