Autumn Sun Tea

Ingredients

  • Boiling Water
  • Rooibos Leaves
  • Orange Zest
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Place Roobibos Leaves & Orange Zest in your tea bag or strainer.
  2. Pour boiling water over tea and steap for 4 minutes.
  3. Remove tea leaves and sprinkle light Cinnamon on top
  4. Enjoy with friends if possible.
